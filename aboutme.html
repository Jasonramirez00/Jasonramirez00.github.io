This is me.

I think growing up in NYC has given me the opportunity to meet a variety of different people from a variety of different backgrounds. This upbringing has taught me how to adapt to new environments and how to collaborate with people who may not share the same views as me. This city has also introduced to many programs that I have utilized to propel myself towards success. Furthermore, in such a vibrate and spontaneous city, there is always something new to witness at every corner.

Since the 4th grade, I have been part of an amazing program called Project Morry. Project Morry gives minorities from underserved communities the ability to have a Summer camp experience, while also supporting them in their academic life. This program has connected me with a worldful of amazing people that have worked as my network of support.

Currently, I am also an intern with NYONTECH, a non-profit helping kids break into the tech field. I attend workshops with NYOTECH where we work on our web development and web design skills. I also go to technology events with NYONTECH where I make connections with others.
