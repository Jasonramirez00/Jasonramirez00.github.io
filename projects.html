When: Summer of 2017
Supplies: RaspberryPi, Ultrasonic Distance Sensors, Piezo Buzzers
Language: Python
Goal: My partner and I wanted to create a device that would allow for visually impaired people to navigate better through the use of sonar technology. The distance sensors triggered buzzers when a wall was within 1.2 meters of the user. Then, the device signaled to the user if they could turn left or right.
